York.games

What is York.games?

York.games is a gameserver network available to any students currently studying at the university of York.
Setup by Hacksoc and managed by both Hacksoc and Shocksoc, we aim to give our members a safe and expansive network to game on.


What kind of servers do you host?

Basically, whatever you want us to host. The servers are split 50/50 between Shocksoc & Hacksoc so typically members get priority
but with enough requests we would be happy to host a server depending on the demand.


Can I get involved?

Absolutely! Currently we need people to create and host events, manage the servers, advertise, etc...
